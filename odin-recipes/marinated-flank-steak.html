Marinated Flank Steak

Description

Marinated flank steak is a flavorful and tender dish that's perfect for a quick and satisfying meal. The key to this recipe is the marinade, which typically consists of a blend of soy sauce, olive oil, garlic, lime juice, and various herbs and spices. This combination not only infuses the meat with a rich, savory flavor but also helps to tenderize the flank steak, which can be a tougher cut. The steak is usually marinated for at least a few hours, or even overnight, to ensure the flavors deeply penetrate the meat.

Once marinated, the flank steak is grilled or broiled to perfection, allowing the exterior to develop a deliciously charred crust while keeping the inside juicy and tender. The steak is often sliced thinly against the grain to enhance its tenderness and is typically served with sides such as grilled vegetables, a fresh salad, or a hearty grain like quinoa or rice. This dish is not only versatile and easy to prepare but also a crowd-pleaser, making it an excellent choice for both family dinners and entertaining guests.

Ingredients

  • ½ cup vegetable oil
  • ⅓ cup low-sodium soy sauce
  • ¼ cup red wine vinegar
  • 2 tablespoons fresh lemon juice
  • 1½ tablespoons Worcestershire sauce
  • 1 tablespoon Dijon mustard
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • ½ teaspoon ground black pepper

Steps

  1. In a bowl, whisk together the vegetable oil, soy sauce, red wine vinegar, lemon juice, Worcestershire sauce, Dijon mustard, garlic, and black pepper.
  2. Place the flank steak in a resealable plastic bag or shallow dish and pour the marinade over it. Seal the bag or cover the dish and refrigerate for at least 2 hours, or overnight for best results.
  3. Preheat your grill or broiler to high heat. Remove the steak from the marinade and discard the marinade.
  4. Grill or broil the steak for about 5-7 minutes per side for medium-rare, or until it reaches your desired level of doneness.
  5. Let the steak rest for 5-10 minutes before slicing it thinly against the grain. Serve with your favorite sides.
