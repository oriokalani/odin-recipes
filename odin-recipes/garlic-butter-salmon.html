Garlic Butter Salmon

Description

Garlic butter salmon is a delightful dish that combines the rich, buttery flavor of salmon with the aromatic essence of garlic. The salmon fillets are typically seared to perfection, creating a crispy exterior while maintaining a tender and juicy interior. The garlic butter sauce, often infused with fresh herbs like parsley or dill, coats the salmon, enhancing its natural flavors and adding a savory depth to the dish. The combination of garlic and butter not only complements the salmon but also elevates it, making it a favorite for both casual dinners and special occasions.

This dish is not only delicious but also quick and easy to prepare, making it a popular choice for home cooks. The preparation involves melting butter in a pan, saut√©ing minced garlic until fragrant, and then cooking the salmon fillets until they reach the desired doneness. The result is a beautifully golden and flavorful piece of fish that pairs well with a variety of sides, such as steamed vegetables, rice, or a fresh salad. Garlic butter salmon is a versatile and satisfying meal that showcases the simplicity and elegance of combining a few high-quality ingredients

Ingredients

  • 4 salmon fillets
  • 3 tablespoons butter
  • 4 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 tablespoon lemon juice
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Steps

  1. Season the salmon fillets with salt and pepper.
  2. Heat butter in a skillet over medium heat and add garlic.
  3. Cook the salmon fillets for 4-5 minutes per side.
  4. Drizzle with lemon juice and serve immediately.
